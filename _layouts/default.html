Biography

Yao Shangshang (Member, IEEE) received a Bachelor's degree in Electronic Information Science and Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2019. In 2021, he obtained a Master's degree from the School of Computer Science at the National University of Defense Technology. Currently, he is an Independent Researcher, engaging in self-directed doctoral-level studies in Computer Science and Technology. His research interests include the design of energy-efficient systems, approximate arithmetic units, and hardware accelerators for AI and machine learning algorithms.
